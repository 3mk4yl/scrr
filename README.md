# Strategic Cyber Risk Reports — Research Archive

This repository hosts downloadable research packs referenced in the
**Strategic Cyber Risk Report** series.

Each pack contains the original research reports (PDF) used as source
material for the corresponding publication.

---

## Structure

reports/YEAR/SCRR_VolXX_ReportYY_Title_Research-Pack_vZ.Z.zip

---

## Methodology

- Research generated using GPT-Researcher (Detailed mode)
- Sources include academic literature, industry reports, and primary sources
- Reports are published unchanged to ensure transparency and verifiability
